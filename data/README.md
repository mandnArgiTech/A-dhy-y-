## Column references

### sutras.tsv
`global_id | adhyaya | pada | num | text_deva | text_slp1 | sutra_type`
- `global_id`: sequential 1-based (use for C lookups)
- `text_deva`: Devanāgarī (UTF-8, NFC)
- `text_slp1`: SLP1 ASCII (converted from Devanāgarī)
- `sutra_type`: SAMJNA / PARIBHASHA / ADHIKARA / VIDHI / NIYAMA / ATIDESHA / NISHEDHA

### dhatupatha.tsv
`global_id | gana | serial_in_gana | upadesa_deva | upadesa_slp1 | meaning_skt | meaning_en | pada_flag | settva`
- `upadesa_deva`: Devanāgarī aupadeshik (upadesa) form
- `pada_flag`: P=parasmaipada A=ātmanepada U=ubhaya
- `settva`: S=seṭ A=aniṭ V=veṭ

### ganapatha.tsv
`gana_id | gana_name_slp1 | gana_name_deva | sutra_ref | serial | member_slp1 | member_deva | note`
- First 14 rows: `gana_name_slp1 = mAheSvarasUtra` (Māheśvara / Śiva sūtras)

### pratyahara.tsv
`id | name_slp1 | name_deva | expansion_slp1 | expansion_deva | member_count | sutra_ref`
- `name_slp1`: e.g. `ac`, `ik`, `hl` (हल् → `hl` in SLP1)
- `expansion_slp1`: space-separated SLP1 phoneme list
